Travel Insurance

Sharda Travel Management offers a range of travel insurance services to ensure that our clients are protected during our travels. Our insurance coverage includes trip cancellation and interruption, emergency medical and dental, baggage and personal effects, and more. They can provide customized insurance plans to meet the specific needs of our clients. For more information, you can visit our website or contact our customer support team.

Certainly! Sharda Travel Management offers travel insurance services to provide our clients with peace of mind while traveling.

Our insurance coverage includes:

> Trip cancellation and interruption: This coverage protects against financial loss if a trip is cancelled or interrupted due to unforeseen circumstances such as illness, injury, or natural disasters.

> Emergency medical and dental: This coverage provides medical and dental assistance in the event of an emergency while traveling.

> Baggage and personal effects: This coverage protects against loss or damage to luggage and personal belongings while traveling.

> Travel accident: This coverage provides financial assistance in the event of accidental death or dismemberment while traveling.


Sharda Travel Management can also provide customized insurance plans to meet the specific needs of our clients.
